About

I started my crochet journey in fifth grade, when a family member gifted me a beginner's knitting loom. After learning to use it and making a pretty sorry hat, I decided I wanted to learn traditiinal knitting with two needles. It proved to be very difficult for my 12 year old mind. I discovered crochet when I was looking for an easier way to learn knitting, seeing as it only uses one needle, or hook, instead of two. Crochet
